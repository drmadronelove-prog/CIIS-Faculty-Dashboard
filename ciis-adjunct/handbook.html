Key sections at a glance

Employment at-will

Adjunct appointments are at-will: either you or CIIS may end the employment relationship at any time. Appointment is per course and per term and does not guarantee future assignments.

Mandatory trainings

Sexual harassment prevention is required every 2 years. FERPA training is required annually. Complete FERPA before your first class day.

Payroll

Payroll is administered through ContinuumHR / Paychex. Submit onboarding paperwork in Paychex Flex and complete timesheets on schedule.

Direct deposit

Set up direct deposit in Paychex Flex with your bank routing and account details. Allow at least one pay period for changes to take effect.

FERPA overview & acknowledgment

FERPA protects the privacy of student education records. Share student records only with those who have a legitimate educational need, and complete the annual FERPA acknowledgment.

Technology acceptable use

Use CIIS systems and your CIIS email for university business, protect credentials, and safeguard confidential data. Personal email must not be used for university correspondence.

Fraud & whistleblower policy

CIIS prohibits fraud and retaliation. Report suspected fraud or misconduct in good faith through the channels described in the handbook; whistleblowers are protected from retaliation.

These summaries are for quick orientation only. The full handbook PDF governs in all cases.
