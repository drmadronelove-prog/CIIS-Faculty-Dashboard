First steps

Your onboarding checklist, grouped by phase of the semester. Use the role switcher above to highlight the steps that apply to you.

Steps marked with a role tag apply only to that role. Everything else applies to all new faculty and teaching assistants.

Download the fillable checklist (PDF)

Before the semester

  • Confirm course, dates, and times with your co-chair.
  • AdjunctsRead your intent/welcome letter and the CIIS Adjunct Handbook.
  • Core facultyRead the ICP Student Handbook and CIIS Faculty Handbook.
  • Read the ICP syllabus template and review sample syllabi for your courses (SharePoint syllabi folder).
  • Sign your contract.
  • Complete ICP faculty orientation and watch for CIIS new faculty orientation invitations.
  • Begin syllabus preparation — choose required texts.
  • Sign your I-9 and upload required documents.
  • Complete Paychex Flex payroll paperwork.
  • Activate (or confirm activation of) your CIIS email address.
  • Upload book adoptions or your course reader to Akademos.
  • Put required texts on reserve in the library and/or on Canvas.
  • Familiarize yourself with Colleague/Ellucian.
  • Familiarize yourself with Paychex and confirm bank and payment preferences.
  • Complete the Canvas tutorial if needed.
  • Complete FERPA training (required annually).
  • Complete sexual harassment training (required every 2 years).
  • Finalize your course syllabus and send it to your co-chairs and program coordinator.
  • Set up your courses in Canvas.
  • Publish your courses on Canvas.
  • Send a welcome message and first assignments to students at least 2 to 6 weeks before the semester.

During the semester

  • AdjunctsComplete timesheets bimonthly.
  • Grade and provide feedback on student assignments within 3 weeks.
  • Check your CIIS email regularly — core faculty daily; adjuncts at least 3 times per week.
  • Respond to all student emails promptly.
  • Use your CIIS email for all university correspondence — do not use your personal email.
  • Contact the appropriate co-chair promptly if student issues arise, or if you have an illness or emergency.

End of semester

  • Complete clinical evaluation forms (links sent by email near the end of semester).
  • Submit all grades and written feedback by the grade deadline.
  • Input grades in Colleague/Ellucian.
